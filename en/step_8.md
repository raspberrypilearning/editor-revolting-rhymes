## Challenge: Add some things!

- Rather than 'thing' - build a list of things!

- Add a word list variable called 'things'.

- Add some nouns to it.

- Shuffle the list.

- In your final `print`, change `"thing"` to `things.pop()`. You can comment out (or delete) the first two prints.

- Change the range in the for loop to print **up to four** lines of revolting rhyme!
